## Tips for Using the Map

### 1. Start with Overview
- Look at full Kenya to understand country-wide patterns
- Identify your highlighted region quickly

### 2. Check Your Region
- Count bloom events in your area
- Compare intensity with other regions
- Note any high-intensity (red) events nearby

### 3. Explore Details
- Click blooms to see exact intensity
- Check data source for confidence
- Compare your region to neighbors

### 4. Use Statistics
- Monitor total events trend
- Track your region's bloom count
- Watch average intensity changes

### 5. Plan Actions
- Red blooms (>80%): Act immediately
- Orange blooms (60-80%): Monitor closely
- Yellow blooms (<60%): Routine care

## Troubleshooting

### Map Not Loading?
- Wait 2-3 seconds for initial load
- Check internet connection
- Refresh browser if needed

### Markers Jumping Around?
- Should NOT happen anymore! 
- If it does, log out and log back in

### Can't Find Your Region?
- Look for the **darker green circle**
- Find the **ğŸ  home icon** (your farm)

### Popups Not Showing?
